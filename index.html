Introduction:

This tool was created to assist researchers in exploring historic tree data of pre-columbian Ohio. This data can assist researchers in finding the historic locations of many tree types, and how the soil interacts with the growing patterns of these trees.

Tutorial Video

Data Source:

Tree data recorded through 1786 survey data of the greater Ohio region. Locations in which survey markers should be placed were often occupied by trees. Surveyors marked each location by what type of tree was located in the area. Coded into shapefile data by the University of Notre Dame.

Soil data collected through the US Department of Agriculture's Geospatial Data Gateway on the national Soil Survey Geographic Database (SSURGO). Data displayed is Soil Order and Drainage.
